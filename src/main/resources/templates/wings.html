Wings Recipe

Ingredients

  • 800g chicken wings
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • Baking powder
  • 1½ tsp Sichuan peppercorns
  • 1½ tsp chilli flakes
  • 3 tbsp Shaoxing wine
  • 1½ tbsp garlic and ginger paste
  • 3 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1½ tsp sesame oil
  • 3 tbsp palm sugar

Directions:

Heat oven to 160C/140C fan/gas 3.

Place the crabmeat in a bowl and pour the egg mixture over the top. Gently toss or fold the ingredients together, taking care not to break up the lumps of crabmeat.

Toss the chicken in 1 tsp salt and the baking powder – this is the secret to getting crispy skin! Put the wings on a wire rack set over an oven tray. Bake for 30 mins, turning halfway, then turn the heat up to 220C/200C fan/gas 7 and cook for 20 mins more.

Meanwhile:

Toast the peppercorns and chilli flakes until fragrant, about 2 mins, then grind using a pestle and mortar. Tip into a saucepan, then add the rest of the ingredients apart from the chopped peanuts and chilli flakes. Whisk to combine, then cook over a medium heat for 8-10 mins until thickened and bubbling. Set aside.

Finally:

Take the chicken off the rack and tip onto the tray, then coat in the glaze. Return to the oven for 5 mins until bubbling and sticky. Serve the chicken scattered with the peanuts and chilli flakes.

Serves 6 people. Offer chopped peanuts & chilli flakes
